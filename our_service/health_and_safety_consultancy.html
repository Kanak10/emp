Our Services


Health and Safety Consultancy

Protecting your employees.

Protecting your business.

Following correct procedures and complying with Health & Safety regulations protects your employees, your business, your reputation and even your position as a director. We offer bespoke consultancy and training courses delivered by qualified professionals to ensure risk is appropriately managed within your business.

Service Categories

We Beleive In

On-site Consultancy

One of our qualified experts will visit your workplace, carry out a full risk assessment and discuss any aspects of Health & Safety with you and your team.

Learn More

Live Training Courses

We provide a wide range of training courses covering Health & Safety subjects from Asbestos Awareness to Working at Height. A qualified professional trainer will deliver one of our courses, or work with you to develop bespoke content tailored to your business.

Learn More

Online Training Courses

Working with one of our specialist partners we deliver on-line courses in over 20 specialist subjects. Train in your own time on a device of your choice. Certificates are issued upon successful completion of the courses; all of which are CPD accredited and RoSPA approved.
